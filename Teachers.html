Our Teachers

All teachers must meet extremely high standards to become part of the CME Tuition Centre team. We screen all applicants to make sure they have the right qualifications, experience and the right attitude to be a successful teacher. Our rigorous processes are designed to ensure our teachers are of the highest calibre, can be trusted and can make a significant difference to your child’s success.

When you select the CME Tuition Centre, you know your teacher will have passed the four-stage selection process.
