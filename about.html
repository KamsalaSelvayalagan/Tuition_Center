About CME Tuition Center

Who We Are

At CME Tuition Center, we have been dedicated to providing quality education for over 10 years. Our primary mission is to help students achieve their academic goals while fostering a love for learning. We offer personalized coaching in a variety of subjects, ensuring that each student receives the attention they need to succeed.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide our students with tailored tuition programs, delivered by fully qualified and subject specialist teachers. The structured support we provide enables our students to achieve excellent results in their exams and gain the confidence to take on new challenges throughout their lives. We aim to instill a lifelong love of learning and the ability to apply new skills both inside and outside the classroom.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become the leading tuition center in Sri Lanka, known for our commitment to excellence in education and our dedication to the success of every student. We strive to create a nurturing and stimulating learning environment where students can thrive academically and personally.

Our History

CME Tuition Center was founded in 2014 with the goal of providing high-quality, personalized education to students in Sri Lanka. Over the years, we have grown from a small tutoring service into a well-established center known for our exceptional teaching staff and comprehensive educational programs. Our growth has been driven by our unwavering commitment to student success and our passion for education.
